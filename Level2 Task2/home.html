Tribute to Narendra Singh Negi

Narendra Singh Negi is a legendary Garhwali singer known for his soulful and melodious songs. His music has touched the hearts of many and is an integral part of Uttarakhand's cultural heritage.

Born on 12th February 1949 in the Pauri district of Uttarakhand, Narendra Singh Negi has contributed immensely to the promotion of Garhwali music and culture. His songs reflect the beauty and essence of the Garhwal region.

Whether it's his classic folk tunes or his modern compositions, Narendra Singh Negi's music has the power to transport you to the picturesque landscapes of Uttarakhand.

This tribute page is dedicated to the musical genius, Narendra Singh Negi, whose songs have left an indelible mark on the hearts of people in the region and beyond.
